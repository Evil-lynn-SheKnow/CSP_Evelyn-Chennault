Computer Science Principles: Overview

There are many things to learn about computers, and where else better to learn them from than at Computer Science Principles. It is a class taught by Ms. LaRose at the Provo campus of UCAS (Utah County Academy of Sciences).

In Computer Science Principles, or CSP as we students call it, we learn the main things of the computer. Some of these things are terms, parts of the computer, and much more. The biggest thing we are currently learning is the fundementals of HTML and CSS, which are for design layout of webpages and broswers, and such.
