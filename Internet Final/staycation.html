Best Tourist Attractions

National parks are already known for their gorgeous colors within the rocks, but there is another beauty that sandstone gives to us: structure. The most common and beautiful places to visit are the hiking trails, river rafting, and rock climbing. The most iconic places to visit are listed below:

  • Navigating the Narrows
  • Walking Pa'rus Trail
  • Emerald Pools Trail
  • Canyon Overlook Trail
  • West Rim Trail
  • Angels Landing
  • Kayaking Virgin River
  • Climbing Lamb's Knoll
  • Exploring East Zion area
  • Kolob Terrace
  • Ladder Canyon
  • Huntress Canyon
